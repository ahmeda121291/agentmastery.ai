# Boost Your Video Email Campaigns: Insider Tips for Superior Deliverability

Ensuring your cold emails not only reach the inbox but also capture the attention of your audience is more challenging than ever. With the right strategies, however, you can significantly improve the deliverability and effectiveness of your video email campaigns. In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore actionable tips and compare top tools to help you achieve email marketing success.

Video content in emails can drastically increase engagement rates, but its inclusion also presents unique challenges for email deliverability. Let's dive into how you can overcome these hurdles and ensure your emails make the intended impact.

## Why Email Deliverability Matters

Email deliverability is crucial for any marketer looking to leverage cold email campaigns to promote video content. It's not just about avoiding spam filters; it's ensuring your message lands in the primary inbox, where it's most likely to be seen and acted upon. Poor deliverability can lead to wasted efforts, missed opportunities, and a tarnished sender reputation.

### The Role of Video in Email Marketing

Including video in your email marketing strategy can significantly enhance recipient engagement. Videos are more likely to be watched and shared, making them an excellent tool for increasing visibility and connecting with your audience on a deeper level. However, the technical aspects of embedding videos can affect deliverability, making it essential to follow best practices.

## Actionable Checklist for Improving Deliverability

To ensure your video emails not only reach but also resonate with your audience, adhere to the following best practices:

- [ ] Ensure your email list is clean and up-to-date to avoid bounces and spam traps.
- [ ] Optimize your email subject lines and content for engagement, avoiding spammy words that trigger filters.
- [ ] Use a reputable email service provider (ESP) known for high deliverability rates.
- [ ] Embed videos using a thumbnail linked to the video instead of directly embedding to avoid increasing the email's size.
- [ ] Monitor your sending reputation regularly and make adjustments as needed.
- [ ] Conduct A/B testing with your email campaigns to understand what works best for your audience.
- [ ] Utilize authentication protocols like SPF, DKIM, and DMARC to verify your emails.

Following these steps can significantly improve the chances of your video emails reaching the inbox.
