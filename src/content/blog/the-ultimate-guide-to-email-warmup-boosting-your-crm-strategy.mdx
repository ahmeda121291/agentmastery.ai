## Why Email Warmup is Essential for CRM

Email warmup is the process of gradually increasing the volume of emails sent from a new email account or domain to build a positive sending reputation. For CRM strategies, this means ensuring that your carefully crafted emails reach your audience, fostering engagement and driving conversions. Without proper warmup, your emails are at a higher risk of being flagged as spam, diminishing your brand's credibility and wasting valuable CRM efforts.

### Best Practices for Email Warmup

1. **Start Slowly and Steadily Increase Volume**: Begin with a low number of emails and gradually increase the volume over time to avoid triggering spam filters.

2. **Engage with Highly Engaged Contacts First**: Start your warmup by sending emails to contacts who have previously shown high levels of engagement. This increases the likelihood of your emails being opened and replied to, further boosting your sender reputation.

3. **Use Personalized Content**: Personalization not only increases engagement rates but also signals to email providers that your content is valuable and not spam.

4. **Monitor Your Sender Score**: Regularly check your sender score, a numerical representation of your domain's reputation, to gauge the effectiveness of your warmup process.

5. **Adapt Based on Feedback**: Be prepared to adjust your strategy based on the feedback from email service providers and the behavior of your recipients.
